Help & Instructions

How to use:

  1. Navigate to Instagram DMs in your browser
  2. Select a date filter type and date
  3. Click "Activate Navigation" to start
  4. The extension will scroll through your DMs
  5. Messages matching your date filter will be highlighted

Filter Types:

  • Exact Date: Find messages from a specific date
  • Date Range: Find messages within 24 hours of a date
  • Days/Weeks/Months Ago: Find messages from relative time periods

Tips:

  • Use the quick date buttons for common time periods
  • Adjust scroll direction based on your needs
  • Monitor progress in the progress section
  • Stop navigation anytime with the stop button
